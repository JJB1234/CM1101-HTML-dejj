Benefits of volunteering

Volunteering allows you to connect with new people by carrying out group projects which can help you develop social skills. Volunteering can also help you further your career as it can help you to train for a career which you may want in the future. This also gives you volunteering hours which you can be rewarded for and it makes you stand out from other applicants for job interviews.

How we volunteer

In the Feel Good Society, we volunteer in many different ways. We have volunteered at local community centres to paint rooms and clean the local areas. We also carry out seasonal events such as creating shoeboxes around christmas to give to those who need them. We also work together to help each other volunteer and gain awards for carrying out a high quality of work when we are volunteering.

How to Volunteer

At the Feel Good Society, we aim to 'do good' and 'feel good' about the work that we do. Volunteering allows us to do both by carrying out work which well benefit others as well as our society. Whether it is a small talk or a large scale project, we would be glad to have you join us in our goal to 'do good' and 'feel good'.

To volunteer with us, pick up an application form from the RGU: Union and join us each week on Wednesday at 1PM at The Terrace, Union Way. We have weekly meetings and get togethers to plan and discuss future volunteering events and how you can join in on the project. You can also email us or contact us on social media for more information about joining our society as well as joining us during our volunteering events.
